Where in Ottawa, Ontario is the most scenic route for a hike?

What makes Mer Bleue the go-to spot?:

  1. it is extremely quite, making it peaceful to listen to nature in its element.
  2. during the fall, the leaves on the trees are filled with many colours
  3. hiking on this trail is great exercise

There are no downsides to choosing this path to hike. Unless you decide to go during the cold winter or end up being unprepared, lacking the tools needed.

Let's begin by setting the scene of every hikers dream: the picture perfect hike. It is the season of autumn and the morning's weather is much cooler compared to during the day. It's about 10 degrees and sunny, with a little bit of a breeze. No phones, no distractions. Just the sounds of birds chirping and the wind breezing through your hair. You decide to stop at the side of the trail and take a glance at the view. It is a line of trees painted in red, yellow, orange and green colours moving slightly because of the wind.
